More About Me

I am Belicia Fernandes, an enthusiastic individual currently committed to pursuing my Bachelor's in Design, specializing in UI/UX. Driven by an unyielding curiosity and a natural inclination for creativity, I am dedicated to immersing myself in the design realm to make a meaningful contribution. My journey is underpinned by a fervent aspiration not only to excel in this field but also to utilize design as a force for positive transformation. I firmly believe that each pixel, every interaction, possesses the power to shape experiences and forge profound connections between people. Through my academic pursuits and hands-on experiences, I am cultivating a profound comprehension of design principles and user-centric methodologies. My ultimate aim is to harness my skills and enthusiasm to craft solutions that are not just visually appealing but also intuitive, inclusive, and impactful.
